About Near-Earth Objects

What are NEOs?

Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) are asteroids and comets whose orbits bring them close to Earth. They range in size from tiny pebbles to large asteroids several kilometers across.

Why Track Them?

  • Planetary defense
  • Scientific research
  • Space exploration opportunities
  • Understanding solar system formation

Classification

  • Atens: Cross Earth's orbit
  • Apollos: Cross Earth's orbit
  • Amors: Approach but don't cross
  • Potentially Hazardous: Large and close
